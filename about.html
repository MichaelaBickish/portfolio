About Me

I am a full stack software developer. I’ve learned a handful of technologies over the past half year and attended Codeworks, a software development bootcamp in Boise, Idaho. It's rewarding to see the completion of an application after all the work it takes to build out. I especially enjoy pair coding because I always learn so much from collaborating with other developers.

I come from a background in Finance. I was the financial director for a local company that grossed over $1 million in yearly revenue and I handled bimonthly payroll and taxes for over sixty employees across 6 locations. My previous role in management accounting took a lot of organization & high attention to detail. I loved my interactions with customers, fellow employees and enjoyed helping my company reach milestones. But I still found myself missing something between all those numbers and balance sheets. I wanted to learn more and push myself to learn something new. That's when I started dreaming about who I could be as a software developer. I've always held an interest for software development and I learned I could incorporate creativity, problem solving and always have access to additional ongoing learning opportunities. It sounded like the fit I was looking for.

In my last couple positions, I took the initiative to step into additional roles and responsibilities to not only increase my value as an employee but because I simply love learning. I know this makes software development a great fit for me. I’m an organized person, so it’s important to me to always have a clear vision of my goals, both career and personal. I really look forward to growing in my career as a software developer!
